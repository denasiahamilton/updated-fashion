About Us

Updated Fashion is an application that allows its users
to determine their outfits based on their current location
and the city's weather. Additionally, the application recommends
possible color combinations by using input from the user.

To begin, click on the weather flow tab and insert your
zip code. It's as easy as that!

Have a shirt but stuck on what colored pants to wear?
Leave it to Updated Fashion to guide you in the right direction!
